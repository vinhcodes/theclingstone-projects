# Keyboard AI

## About
Keyboard AI is a straightforward text generation app that helps you write personalized content quickly. Just enter your topic or what you want to say, and let AI help you write it better.

## What It Does
• Generates personalized emails based on your needs
• Helps write professional or casual messages
• Creates notes and text content from your prompts
• Makes writing faster and easier on your phone

## Key Features
• Simple prompt-based text generation
• Quick response time
• Works for emails, messages, and notes
• Easy-to-use mobile interface
• No complex settings or learning curve

## How It Helps
• Save time on writing routine emails
• Get help with professional communication
• Write better personal messages
• Turn your ideas into well-written text

## Technical Details
• Built with React Native
• Optimized for mobile use
• Secure and private
• Available for iOS and Android

Simple, focused, and helpful - Keyboard AI is your pocket writing assistant when you need to turn your thoughts into well-written text.
